Who We Are

Nutrition,
No Compromise.

At Nutria, we believe healthy food shouldn't be hard to find. Our smart vending machines are stocked fresh daily with balanced meals, energizing drinks, and wholesome snacks — crafted with real ingredients and zero shortcuts.

Whether you're between meetings, at the gym, or traveling, a Nutria machine is your reliable source of fuel that actually nourishes your body.

  • Freshly prepared meals restocked every day
  • No artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors
  • Transparent nutrition labels on every product
  • Locally sourced ingredients wherever possible
  • Eco-friendly packaging and sustainable operations
